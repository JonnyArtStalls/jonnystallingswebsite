About

I am a Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Music at the University of California San Diego. My research interests lie in developing physics-based sound synthesis techniques for computer animation and augmented reality. Before coming to UC San Diego, I worked as an R&D Scientist at Applied Research in Acoustics and as a freelance developer at Moog Music and Lofelt. As a freelance developer, I wrote algorithms for music synthesizers and headphones, including the Razer Nari Ultimate, the world's first intelligent haptics-enabled gaming headset.
